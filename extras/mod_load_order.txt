# Example GZDoom Load Order (Make It So launcher)
# Save as plain text. Load order is top → bottom.

01. BRUTALPACK 10.4.pk3
    > Core gameplay overhaul (base mod). Adds advanced AI, gore, and shoulder weapons.
02. Brutal Pack Neural Pack.pk3
    > High-res AI-upscaled textures and sprites for Brutal Pack visuals.
03. BP-Glory-Kill-3.pk3
    > Adds cinematic glory-kill executions (v3).
04. BrutalDoom_PB-Blade custom.pk3
    > Custom melee system and blade weapon variant for PB/BP compatibility.
05. autoautosave-v1.6.3.pk3
    > Automatically creates rolling autosaves during gameplay.
06. Behold_BP_Main.pk3
    > Resurrection and crowd-control addon for Brutal Pack.
      Default configuration:
        - Earthquake preset: Whiplash
        - Spawn mode: Random Mayhem
        - Message style: HUD / Bold
      Must load *after* Brutal Pack to hook into its shoulder-weapon logic,
      resurrection system, and monster definitions.
      Forty variants are available for alternate quake, message, and spawn styles.
07. CorruptionCards-v6.3b.pk3
    > Random gameplay modifiers that add roguelike twists to each map.
08. Crosshair Enemy HP Bar.pk3
    > Displays enemy health directly under the crosshair.
09. flashlight_plus_plus_v9_1.pk3
    > Enhanced flashlight with adjustable beam and flicker.
10. gearbox-0.7.3.pk3
    > Custom weapon and upgrade selector; integrates with most weapon mods.
11. GunBonsai-0.10.6.pk3
    > Weapon XP and perk system that evolves your guns over time.
12. GZ-WalkItOut.pk3
    > Fitness-style movement tracker (encourages constant movement).
13. LiveReverb.pk3
    > Dynamic environmental reverb based on sector type and geometry.
14. minimap_m.pk3
    > Adds an overlay minimap with custom colors and transparency options.
15. target-spy-v2.0.1.pk3
    > Displays detailed info about enemies, damage type, and distance.
16. Optional HUD Addon - HXRTCHUD_BP_V10.4v2 El Donte.pk3
    > Alternate HUD layout tuned for Brutal Pack 10.4.

# Notes:
# - Load order is top → bottom (higher numbers override earlier ones).
# - Behold_BP_Main.pk3 replaces older behold_inv_* versions.
# - All other mods are optional but tested compatible with BP 10.4 + Behold BP Edition.
